## Data Representation

Data Representation is a tool and technique used in 11 processes (PMBOK6). Based on the process name, context of the process it can mean any of the following techniques.

1. [Affinity diagrams](/pmbok6/affinity-diagrams)
2. [Cause-and-effect diagrams](/pmbok6/cause-and-effect-diagrams)
3. [Control charts](/pmbok6/control-charts)
4. [Flowcharts](/pmbok6/flowcharts)
5. [Hierarchical charts](/pmbok6/hierarchical-charts)
6. [Histograms](/pmbok6/histograms)
7. [Logical data model](/pmbok6/logical-data-model)
8. [Matrix diagrams](/pmbok6/matrix-diagrams)
9. [Matrix-based charts](/pmbok6/matrix-based-charts)
10. [Mind mapping](/pmbok6/mind-mapping)
11. [Probability and impact matrix](/pmbok6/probability-and-impact-matrix)
12. [Scatter diagrams](/pmbok6/scatter-diagrams)
13. [Stakeholder engagement assessment matrix](/pmbok6/stakeholder-engagement-assessment-matrix)
14. [Stakeholder mapping/ representation](/pmbok6/stakeholder-mapping/-representation)
15. [Text-oriented formats](/pmbok6/text-oriented-formats)
