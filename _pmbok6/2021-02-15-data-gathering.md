## Data Gathering

Data Gathering is a tool and technique used by the project team. Based on the process name and process context it can mean any of the following.

1. [Benchmarking](/pmbok6/benchmarking)
2. [Brainstorming](/pmbok6/brainstorming)
3. [Check-sheets](/pmbok6/check-sheets)
4. [Checklists](/pmbok6/checklists)
5. [Focus-groups](/pmbok6/focus-groups)
6. [Interviews](/pmbok6/interviews)
7. [Market-research ](/pmbok6/market-research-)
8. [Questionnaires-and-surveys](/pmbok6/questionnaires-and-surveys)
9. [Statistical-sampling](/pmbok6/statistical-sampling)

**Data Gathering tool and technique is used by project team during following proceses.**

1. [Develop Project Charter](/pmbok6/develop-project-charter)
2. [Develop Project Management Plan](/pmbok6/develop-project-management-plan)
3. [Collect Requirements](/pmbok6/collect-requirements)
4. [Plan Quality Management](/pmbok6/plan-quality-management)
5. [Manage Quality](/pmbok6/manage-quality)
6. [Control Quality](/pmbok6/control-quality)
7. [Identify Risks](/pmbok6/identify-risks)
8. [Perform Qualitative Risk Analysis](/pmbok6/perform-qualitative-risk-analysis)
9. [Perform Quantitative Risk Analysis](/pmbok6/perform-quantitative-risk-analysis)
10. [Plan Risk Responses](/pmbok6/plan-risk-responses)
11. [Plan Procurement Management](/pmbok6/plan-procurement-management)
12. [Identify Stakeholders](/pmbok6/identify-stakeholders)
13. [Plan Stakeholder Engagement](/pmbok6/plan-stakeholder-engagement)
