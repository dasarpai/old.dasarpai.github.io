## Interpersonal and Team Skills

“Interpersonal and Team Skills” is one of the most powerful tools and techniques of a project manager. This is used in 20 processes of PMBOK 6th Edition. PMBOK standard expects a project manager to be aware of these techniques and practice them effectively whenever the of these arises in a project life cycle. Interpersonal and Team Skills means following skills.

1. [Active listening](/pmbok6/active-listening)
2. [Communication styles assessment](/pmbok6/communication-styles-assessment)
3. [Conflict management](/pmbok6/conflict-management)
4. [Cultural awareness ](/pmbok6/cultural-awareness-)
5. [Decision making](/pmbok6/decision-making)
6. [Emotional intelligence](/pmbok6/emotional-intelligence)
7. [Facilitation](/pmbok6/facilitation)
8. [Influencing](/pmbok6/influencing)
9. [Leadership](/pmbok6/leadership)
10. [Meeting management](/pmbok6/meeting-management)
11. [Motivation](/pmbok6/motivation)
12. [Negotiation](/pmbok6/negotiation)
13. [Networking](/pmbok6/networking)
14. [Nominal group technique](/pmbok6/nominal-group-technique)
15. [Observation/ conversation](/pmbok6/observation/-conversation)
16. [Political awareness](/pmbok6/political-awareness)
17. [Team building](/pmbok6/team-building)
