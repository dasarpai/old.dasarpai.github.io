## Executing Process Group

Executing Process Group has the following 10 processes in PMBOK 6th Edition.

1. [Direct and Manage Project Work](/pmbok6/direct-and-manage-project-work)
2. [Manage Project Knowledge](/pmbok6/manage-project-knowledge)
3. [Manage Quality](/pmbok6/manage-quality)
4. [Acquire Resources](/pmbok6/acquire-resources)
5. [Develop Team](/pmbok6/develop-team)
6. [Manage Team](/pmbok6/manage-team)
7. [Manage Communications](/pmbok6/manage-communications)
8. [Implement Risk Responses](/pmbok6/implement-risk-responses)
9. [Conduct Procurements](/pmbok6/conduct-procurements)
10. [Manage Stakeholder Engagement](/pmbok6/manage-stakeholder-engagement)
